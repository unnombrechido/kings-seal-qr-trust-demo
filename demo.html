About King's Seal

At King’s Seal, we strive to create a secure environment for QR code users. Our mission is to protect individuals from phishing attacks and ensure that legitimate creators can share their content safely.

Inspired by physical mail, King's Seal uses three layers of trust: Personal Trust, Official Seal, and Caution. No complex crypto for casual use—strong protection where it matters.
